About WaxSpa

Why wax spa

Wax Spa has been providing clients with the most advanced skincare and wax treatments in the Miami area since 2006 , and our customer-centered approach continues to garner high praise. Our expert estheticians use only the highest quality European wax, and their attention to detail keeps Wax Spa at the top of being the best in countless categories. From the moment you walk into Wax Spa, you will feel a warmth and ease that is often lacking in larger spas where clients are just another number. We want our clients to be part of the Miami Wax Spa family for the duration, and our philosophy is quite simple: quality over quantity.

Immaculate hygene for your safety

We are also obsessive when it comes to cleanliness and hygiene and we never recycle our wax or spatulas, creating a truly contamination free hair removal center. The result is long-lasting silky smooth skin that will give you an all-over glow from head-to-toe.

Feels like home!

Your ultimate comfort and satisfaction are our top priorities, and the Wax Spa staff is completely dedicated to making your experience one to remember. Our award-winning formula of top-notch customer service together with our proprietary waxing technique, competitive pricing and complete hygiene is often replicated, but never duplicated. We have no doubt that after just one visit, you will become part of the Wax Spa family for life!
